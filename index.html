The Sopranos

    • Gabagool! What a great show. That Tony Soprano has so many layers. Is he bad? Is he good? Does it matter? Top tier prestige television.

I Think You Should Leave with Tim Robinson

  • A newer sketch comedy show from former Saturday Night Live writer, Tim Robinson. While it may not be on any best of lists yet, Robinson is ushering in a new era of sketch comedy.

Buffy the Vampire Slayer

  • Classic late 90's supernatural drama for the teens (or tweens). Based on the film of the 80s, Buffy just wants to be a normal teen, but that ain't happening! Action! Drama! Forbidden love AND a musical? Sign me up!
